Transforming Skill Development in Media and Entertainment

The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, through the Media and Entertainment Skill Council (MESC), presents the Training of Trainer (ToT) programs. These initiatives are designed to enhance the proficiency and capabilities of trainers, ensuring they can effectively guide and mentor the youth, thereby improving employability and engagement.

To enhance employability in the Indian workforce and provide gainful engagement for dropouts, it’s crucial to have a pool of trainers capable of guiding the youth effectively. The ToT program is instrumental in achieving this goal.

Discover the Benefits of our ToT Program

  • Certification on Domain Skills: Trainers receive a certification recognized across the nation, aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF), and awarded by Skill India and NSDC.

  • Interactive Learning: The program emphasizes group activities to foster collaborative learning.

  • Real-Time Demonstrations: Trainers will experience practical demonstrations on the use of various equipment, safety, and health practices.

  • Industry Expert Guidance: Opportunity for in-person guidance from industry experts.

  • Training on relevant NOS of the given Job Role

  • Use of Interactive Platforms: Incorporates various interactive tools and platforms for effective demonstrations.

  • Enhanced Digital Literacy: Opportunity to enhance digital literacy skills.
