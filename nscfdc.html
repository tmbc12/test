National Schedule Caste Finance and Development Corporation

According to the 2011 census, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes comprise 16.6% and 8.6% of India's population, respectively. In 2018, MESC joined forces with the National Scheduled Caste Finance and Development Corporation (NSCFDC) with a shared mission to empower Scheduled Castes by enhancing access to financial resources, promoting skill development, and implementing innovative initiatives that foster economic prosperity and social inclusivity.

To be eligible for this scheme, beneficiaries must belong to the Scheduled Castes Community and meet the following income criteria:

  • - Annual family income not exceeding Double the Poverty Line (DPL), currently:

  • - Rs 1,20,000/- per annum for urban areas

  • - Rs 1,20,000/- per annum for urban areas

NSFDC provides term loans covering up to 90% of the project cost, subject to the following conditions:

  • - Scheduled Caste Associations (SCAs) must contribute their share of assistance as per their schemes

  • - Required subsidy must be provided

  • - Financial resources must be tied up from other available sources

In the FY 2019-2020, MESC achieved a remarkable milestone by training over 660 candidates and certifying more than 550 individuals, empowering them with skills and knowledge to attain economic self-reliance and independence.
