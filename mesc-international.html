MESC International

MESC International is dedicated to promoting collaboration between Indian and international trainers for a dynamic exchange of expertise. By upskilling Indian resources through exposure to international standards and practices, the Council grooms talents by leveraging the knowledge of global industry experts. MESC International is also focused on establishing Centers of Excellence in different countries to promote skill development and industry-aligned training programs. Additionally, the Council explores collaborative research projects on emerging technologies, trends, and innovations in the Media & Entertainment sector.

Expanding Global Skill Ecosystems

MESC International strategically bridges the global skill gap by deploying and developing skilled resources, offering expert consultancy, and cultivating international partnerships. Our goal is to elevate the Media & Entertainment sector globally through sustainable skill ecosystems, fostering innovation and raising industry standards. Join us in transforming the future of media and entertainment education.

Global Skill Alliance

MESC creates skill ecosystems in partner countries through skilling, upskilling, and reskilling initiatives. Indian skilled resources are deployed as trainers, master trainers, and project executives to share their expertise.


Consulting & Partnering

MESC offers consultancy to host countries, ministries, and departments to nurture skill development and the (M&E) industry.The Council supports the growth of the M&E industry in host countries through Indian studios, production houses, and organizing industry events.

Project Outsourcing

The Council develops content for global audiences through partner studios and facilitates outsourcing of Indian projects (IP) to the global market.MESC assists M&E studios and production houses in setting up production facilities and offices in partner countries.

Universal Skill Certificate

MESC offers Skill India certifications recognized by the Indian government and esteemed by international universities, boosting credibility and employability globally. These certifications provide students with a competitive advantage and expanded career opportunities worldwide.
