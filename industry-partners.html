Industry Partners

MESC is proud to have partnered with India's leading national, regional, and global industry bodies and associations, who have been constantly involved in supporting and strengthening the Media and Entertainment ecosystem. These Industry Partners collaborate with MESC in its work to improve sustainability best practices in the sector.

FICCI ENTERTAINMENT DIVISION

WWW.FICCI.IN

Established in 1927, FICCI is the largest and oldest apex business organisation in India. The Media & Entertainment Division of FICCI serves a vital link between the M & E Industry and Information & Broadcasting Ministry. The division is guided by the Chairman Mr. Uday Shankar and Co-Chairman Mr Ramesh Sippy.

FILM & TV PRODUCER’S GUILD

WWW.FILMTVGUILDINDIA.ORG

The Film & Television Producers Guild of India Limited is an autonomous non-profit premier film trade body comprising of industry stakeholders, is to be a principal negotiator with the Government on various critical issues, resolving intra and external trade disputes of the industry.

INDIAN OUTDOOR ADVERTISER’S ASSOCIATION

WWW.IOAA.CO.IN

The only National Organization of the out-of-home advertising media industry in India. IOAA was formed and registered as a not-for-profit company under the Companies Act in 2007. Our members roster consists of all large media owners with pan – India presence .

INDIAN BROADCASTING FOUNDATION

WWW.IBFINDIA.COM

Twenty five years after the Indian economy opened up to align with a shrinking world and the broadcasting sector moved from the state-owned Doordarshan to almost 900 channels now, television has indeed played a major role in shaping the viewer’s choices and educating the masses.

FILM FEDERATION OF INDIA

WWW.FILMFED.ORG

Formed to promote commerce in general and in particular to protect & watch over the interests of the Indian Film Industry & allied industries and trades, including the interests of producers, distributors and-exhibitors of films and of all others connected with the Film Industry, to regulate their method or doing business.

ASSOCIATION OF RADIO OPERATORS OF INDIA

WWW.AROI.IN

Association of Radio Operators for India (AROI) is the official association of the private commercial radio stations in India. Through a collective consultative and democratic process, it defines and reflects the common policies and goals of the private radio industry in India.
