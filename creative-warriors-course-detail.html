Online Acting Classes by Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Course Description

Nawazuddin Siddiqui started his acting career with a half-a-minute role in the movie, Sarfarosh. Today, he is one of the most sought-after actors in India and has performed well in almost all genres of movies, from Gangs of Wasseypur to Thackeray. As a trained actor from the National School of Drama (NSD), he uses advanced acting techniques to portray complex characters. In his first-ever acting class, Nawazuddin shows how research is at the core of every great performance, how to bring real-life experiences into every role and how to build your own characters.

What you'll learn in this course?

In this course, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, a renowned actor trained at the National School of Drama (NSD), shares his experties in portraying different characters. Drawing inspiration from his various roles in films like Gangs of Wasseypur to Thackerey, Nawazuddin guides students through his detailed lessons.

  • Learn advanced acting techniques to portray complex characters

  • How to research to enhance your acting performance and make your characters more real

  • Bring real-life experiences into your acting role

  • How to build up your character and develop them

  • Insights on performing across various genres

  • Tips and advice on navigating the acting industry and building a successful acting career
