Manthan - The Impeccable Academia

Manthan is an esteemed event organized by the Media & Entertainment Skills Council (MESC), aimed at fostering collaboration and innovation within the Media & Entertainment (M&E) sector. This platform brings together academia, vocational partners, universities, colleges, and schools to discuss and strategize on advancing education and training in M&E roles.

Creating Sector Awareness

Highlighting the crucial role of M&E in the global economy and its potential for growth.



Accessible Education for All

Exploring job roles eligible for government incentives and programs to boost industry participation.


Expansion of Training Centers

Encouraging partners to establish more centers for training in specialized M&E job roles.

Long-Term Educational Partnerships

Collaborating on sustainable programs that align with industry needs and educational standards.

Apprenticeship Opportunities

Facilitating the enrollment of certified candidates into apprenticeship programs within the M&E sector.

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

Discussing pathways for individuals to gain recognition for existing skills and experiences in the M&E field.
